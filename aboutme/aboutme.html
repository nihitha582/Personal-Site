About Me:

Hello! My name is Nihitha Pinnamaraju, but most people just call me Nihi. I am currently a rising Junior studying Computer Science in the College of Literature, Science, and the Arts at the University of Michigan. I am also applying to the Minor in Business at the Ross School of Business. From a young age itself, I have had an interest in both tech and business, and while I have had experiences in each field separately, I want to be able to combine my two passions in my future professional career.

Whatever I may do in the future, I want to be able to make an impact in tech industry, especially when it comes to women. I not only want to put my Computer Science knowledge to use in order to advance programs, but I also want to inspire and motivate women to step out and shine as well. I hope through my years at the University of Michigan, my internship experiences, and the various people I will meet, that I can make my vision come true!

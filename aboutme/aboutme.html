About Me:

Hello! My name is Nihitha Pinnamaraju, but I mostly just go by Nihi. I am currently a rising Junior studying Computer Science in the College of Literature, Science, and the Arts at the University of Michigan. This upcoming summer I will be interning at Nike Technology in Beaverton, OR. I am excited to have this opportunity because Nike isn't the traditional tech company, and I can't wait to experience how they operate within the tech industy and be able to contribute to that innovation.

Whatever I may do in the future, I want to be able to make an impact in the tech industry, especially when it comes to women. I not only want to put my Computer Science knowledge to use in order to advance programs, but I also want to inspire and motivate women to step out and shine as well. I hope through my years at the University of Michigan, my internship experiences, and the various people I will meet, that I can make my vision come true!

Aside from academics and my professional interests, I love being active and outdoors. I have been to around ten national parks and have hiked some gorgeous trails. One of my side goals is to hopefully be able to visit all of the national parks. Along with being outdoors and traveling, I also enjoy video editing. I may not be the best at it, but I hope that once I get to travel more, I can pick up a few editing tricks and film secrets and create some stunning content. I have a few videos up on youtube, so if you want to check them out, then be sure to follow the link on the Contact Me page!
